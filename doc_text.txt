INTRODUCTION

The promoter of the company had started the construction work in 2005 as proprietor concern in the name of NOAH CONSTRUCTIONS and successfully executed number of projects for individuals and corporate reputed companies.
The Company had started in 2005 and since then had vastly executed projects  in Secunderabad and Hyderabad in various residential and commercial projects covering all parts of city. The process of growth into a qualitative and customised construction continues more and more experienced personnel in the construction industry and delivering high quality commitment with timely execution.
M/s. Noah Constructions had made progressive and steady expansion in last 15 years and undertaken various projects with utmost customer satisfaction with quality checks and well handled professional and technical expertise.
The company had the involvement of large number of Engineers, Technicians Skilled and unskilled for the success implementation projects.
It has panelled with Architects , Engineers , Contractors and advisory board committee for smooth functioning with skilled technical expertise to meet the standards of Civil Engineering. It has continuously maintaining the coordination of vendors , Suppliers , Materials , Labour contractors , centering Labour and all required machinery related to construction.
